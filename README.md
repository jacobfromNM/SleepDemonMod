**Versions:** Mod Available for Minecraft 1.20.1 and 1.19.2

**Author:** jacobfromnm

### **Overview**

The Click mod adds an eerie and primarily ambient entity to Minecraft. Inspired by the movie "Hereditary", this mod introduces a demonic and unseen entity - the demon king Paimon, that produces an unsettling (and perhaps familiar) sound that echoes through forests, caves, and dark corners. This mod pairs nicely with the [Sound Physics Remastered](https://www.curseforge.com/minecraft/mc-mods/sound-physics-remastered) mod!

### **Features:**

*   **Unsettling tongue click sound from the film:** Plays around players in caves, shaded forests, and other dark places.
*   **Invisible Stalker:** A hidden entity spawns with the sound, pursuing the player for a short period before vanishing into the shadows.

### **Spoiler / Hints**

<div class="spoiler">The entity is completely invisible in dark spaces, but can be heard approaching the player before causing damage (nothing too severe, but can mean the difference between life/death in some situations). The entity is designed to offer more of a brief ambient horror type experience (rather than your usual "dweller" type mod - which there are plenty of), and layers nicely with other horror/psychological type mods.</div>

Feedback/Suggestions are welcome - thanks for checking it out, I hope it adds to your experience!

### **Known Bugs:**

*   Regarding the config, setting the spawnTickInterval too low in combination with the spawnChance being too high may cause crashes and an uncomfortable amount of audio.
*   I've tested as much as possible in my down time, but please let me know if you encounter any bugs and I'll do my best to get to them quickly. Thank you for checking out my mods!
