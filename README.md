**Versions:** Minecraft 1.20.x & 1.19.x **Author:** jacobfromnm

### **Overview**

> _"Beware the hour when dream and shadow entwine. The Sleep Demon is said to seek those who slumber unguarded, wrenching them violently from safety and into shadow…"_

On rare occasions, attempting to sleep may instead trigger a harrowing experience at the hands of a dark force - seeking to unexpectedly disorient, and place the player in harm's way.

### **Features:**

*   When a player lies down to sleep, there is a configurable chance (default 20%) that the Sleep Demon will interrupt your slumber.
*   Darkness envelops the player when teleported.
*   The player is teleported to a random, distant location (within a configurable range).
*   The player's health is reduced to one heart.
*   A randomly selected, unsettling red message appears at the center of the screen, hinting at the demon's presence.

### **Configuration file lets you adjust:**

*   Sleep Demon trigger chance
*   Minimum and maximum teleport distance
*   Duration of the darkness effect (in ticks)
*   Whether sounds should play
*   Whether debug logging is enabled for troubleshooting.
